About AIS


At AIS, we pride ourselves on having a solid and high quality education. We supplement school activities with a wide range of extracurricular activities such as sports, languages and creativity. We also help our students develop positive personal qualities and values so that they grow up to be balanced, principled, independent adults who contribute to society.



Benefits of our school

a large area of the school with a developed high-tech infrastructure

modern and advanced training equipment

2 swimming pools and 3 gyms for the physical development of children

more than a hundred equipped class rooms

qualified and competent teaching staff
